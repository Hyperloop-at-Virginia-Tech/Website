About Hyperloop

Hyperloop is a technology that was first proposed by entrepreneur Elon Musk in August of 2013. The proposition outlines specifications for a transport framework that utilizes vacuum tunneling to enable mass transport at speeds in excess of 760 miles per hour. This will enable trips such as LA to San Francisco in under half an hour.

Transport by Vacuum

Operating the pods in a vacuum reduces the amount of air resistance encountered thus enabling rapid acceleration.
