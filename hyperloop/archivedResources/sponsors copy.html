SUB TEAMS

Hyperloop at Virginia Tech is comprised of several separate sub-teams which work in conjunction to develop a functional pod

Controls

The controls team is responsible for developing the software and hardware systems that manage the pod

Business

The business team is responsible for marketing the team name, acquiring sponsorships, and managing team budgets

Propulsion

As the name implies, the propulsion team is tasked with accelerating our pod from a stand still to over 300mph

Braking

Within the span of a 1 mile test track, our pod must accelerate from 0 to 300mph and back to 0, braking is responsible for stopping the pod

Chasis and Aeroshell

The Chasis and Aeroshell team is both literally and figuratively the glue that holds our pod together

Suspension

At 300mph, it is critical that our pod's suspension is able to withstand significant forces
